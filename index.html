PyData Chicago

PyData Chicago is an open source platform to share Data Analytics/Science and Python related knowledge. Our events are advertised to our 3000+ Meetup members and generally attract between 50-80 attendees. Our regular meetups are scheduled for the first Monday of every month (excepting holidays) from 6:00 to 8:00 pm. We are currently hosted by Arity in Merchandise Mart. In addition to bringing great speakers to our community of data enthusiasts, we also aim to support the Chicago Data Community at large.
